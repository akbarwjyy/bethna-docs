## Trading

### How do I start trading?
1. Connect wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
2. Deposit USDC on Base
3. Chat with AI: "I'm bullish on ETH"
4. Review recommendation and approve

### What fees does BethNa charge?
- **Free Tier**: 20% performance fee on profits
- **Bronze-Platinum**: 5-15% fees (stake $BETH for discounts)
- **No fees on losses**

### Can I lose more than I invest?
No. Options buyers can only lose the premium paid (100% max loss).
