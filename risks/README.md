# Risk Disclosure

## Important Notice

**BethNa AI Trader is experimental software. Trading options carries significant risk. You could lose your entire investment.**

## Categories of Risk

### 1. Financial Risks

Options trading is inherently risky:
- **Total Loss**: Options can expire worthless
- **Leverage**: Options provide leverage, amplifying losses
- **Volatility**: Prices can move rapidly against you
- **Theta Decay**: Time value erodes, even if you're directionally correct

**Risk Level**: **HIGH**

### 2. Technical Risks

Smart contracts and AI systems can fail:
- **Smart Contract Bugs**: Exploits, reentrancy, access control issues
- **Oracle Failures**: Incorrect price feeds
- **Backend Downtime**: Service interruptions
- **AI Errors**: Incorrect signals, model drift

**Risk Level**: **MEDIUM-HIGH**

### 3. Regulatory Risks

Crypto regulations are evolving:
- **Jurisdictional Restrictions**: Services may be restricted in your location
- **Tax Implications**: Trading profits may be taxable
- **Compliance Changes**: Future regulations may impact operations

**Risk Level**: **MEDIUM**

### 4. Liquidity Risks

Markets may lack liquidity:
- **Slippage**: Actual execution worse than quoted
- **Unable to Exit**: Can't sell positions quickly
- **Market Impact**: Large trades move the market

**Risk Level**: **MEDIUM**
